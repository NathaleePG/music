Fav Song of August 2017

August 17, 2017

Beauty and the Beast - John Legend & Ariana Grande
.
.

Being a pretty big Disney fan of the original Beauty and the Beast, I have to admit that I still have yet to watch the live version of this movie. I know I’m pretty late but I’m actually trying to find the perfect time to watch it with Anmol. I have to say though, that I’m completely obsessed with this song and it still never fails to sound so magical even a few months after the hype. It’s such a pretty modern take of the original song, bringing me back to that princessy dreamy state and of course, those funny childhood memories. I listen to this every time I have that desire to feel an emotional type of vibe, lol! You know what I mean? Ladies?

What song brings the vibes this month?
